# Understanding Contract Law Basics

Contract law forms the foundation of modern business relationships. In this article, we'll explore the essential elements that make a contract valid and enforceable.

## What Makes a Contract Valid?

A valid contract requires several key elements:

1. Offer and Acceptance
2. Consideration
3. Intention to Create Legal Relations
4. Capacity to Contract
5. Legality of Purpose
